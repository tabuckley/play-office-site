Bringing “Nazar” to Cairo — Lara Kobeissi at D-CAF

2 November 2024

This November, we were excited to support artist Lara Kobeissi as she brought her captivating multisensory performance, Nazar, to the Downtown Contemporary Arts Festival (D-CAF) in Cairo.

Lara was one of the talented artists supported through the Resonate programme, where she developed her unique artistic voice and experimented with new technologies like XR. We are incredibly proud of how she has evolved as an artist, and Nazar represents the culmination of her exploration into the intersection of tradition and innovation.

About Nazar

Nazar is an immersive live performance incorporating virtual reality and interactive storytelling, transporting audiences into a distant future where they explore the excavated home of a virtual dweller in search of personalised Evil Eye treatments. Through visuals, sound, smell, taste, and touch, the performance weaves a ritualistic act of soft resistance, restoring the body, collective memory, and intangible heritage.
