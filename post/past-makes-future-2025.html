Past Makes Future 2025

2 November 2024

We are excited to announce that Past Makes Future will return on February 10th, 2025, at the Portsmouth Guildhall — this time with a more expansive and international focus.

Sponsored by Bored Peach Club, this year's conference will explore new frontiers in art and heritage through emerging technologies, with discussions led by international artists and innovators pushing the boundaries of creativity and cultural preservation.

Building on 2024

Following the success of the 2024 edition, which featured key figures from the Royal Shakespeare Company, Aspex Portsmouth, and the Centre for Creative and Immersive Extended Reality, Past Makes Future 2025 aims to broaden the conversation by inviting global voices into the dialogue.

We invite artists, technologists, heritage professionals, and cultural thinkers to join us for what promises to be a dynamic day of discussion, exploration, and networking.
