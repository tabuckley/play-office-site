Past events & workshops 2023–2025

A record of Play Office workshops, public events, exhibitions and programmes since 2023. Showing past events demonstrates the breadth and consistency of Play Office's public delivery.

2025

  • ExhibitionAspex Portsmouth

    In Touch, In Ruin — Resonate II exhibition

    Group exhibition featuring Resonate II artists Hannah Mae Buckingham, Harry Payne and James Wylie. Presented at Aspex Portsmouth.

  • InternationalCairo, Egypt

    Resonate II Touring Open Studio — Cairo

    Play Office travels to Cairo to present Resonate II at Medrar for Contemporary Art, part of an international exchange programme.

  • InternationalBeirut Art Centre

    AlterCare: A Worldbuilding Workshop

    XR workshop at Beirut Art Centre exploring ecologies, worldbuilding and emerging technologies in collaboration with local artists.

  • ConferencePortsmouth Guildhall

    Past Makes Future 2025

    An international micro-conference exploring heritage, technology, memory and artistic practice at Portsmouth Guildhall. Brought together artists, researchers, heritage professionals and cultural organisations.

  • WorkshopPlay Office, Portsmouth

    Puppetry and XR Theatre Making with Phoebe Hyder

    Hands-on session blending traditional puppetry craft with XR tools, led by theatre-maker Phoebe Hyder.

  • WorkshopPlay Office, Portsmouth

    Digital Sculpting and AR with Livi Wilmore

    Browser-based 3D modelling and augmented reality workshop led by artist Livi Wilmore, exploring accessible creative technology tools.

  • InternationalAustin, Texas, USA

    SXSW — Sharing Play Office with Austin

    Play Office presented at South by Southwest, introducing the project and its community model to an international audience.

2024

  • WorkshopPlay Office, Portsmouth

    Clay, Photogrammetry and Animation

    Full creative pipeline workshop: clay modelling, 3D scanning with Polycam, rigging in Adobe Mixamo, and publishing to WebXR via STYLY. Led by Tom Ward.

  • WorkshopPlay Office, Portsmouth

    TouchDesigner and Projection Mapping

    Sold-out workshop exploring audio-reactive visuals and projection mapping. Participants created live visual systems using TouchDesigner.

  • WorkshopPlay Office, Portsmouth

    VR Painting and 3D Modelling with Open Brush

    Immersive workshop exploring VR painting and 3D spatial modelling using Open Brush, led by artist Tom Ward.

  • InternationalCairo, Egypt

    Bringing “Nazar” to Cairo — Lara Kobeissi at D-CAF

    Play Office supports Resonate artist Lara Kobeissi as she brings her multisensory performance to the Downtown Contemporary Arts Festival in Cairo.

  • Artist DevelopmentPlay Office, Portsmouth

    Resonate I — Artist Development Programme

    The first Resonate cohort: five groups of artists exploring memory and heritage through immersive technologies.

2023

  • LaunchPlay Office, Portsmouth

    Play Office Launch

    Play Office opens its doors at The Old Pay Office, Portsmouth Historic Quarter. Attended by MP Stephen Morgan, Council Leader Steve Pitt, and artists and community members from across Portsmouth.
