About

An incubation space for creatives

Play Office aims to explore local heritage as inspiration for placemaking and artistic works — a space for creatives to develop dynamic, future-focused, responsive practices.

Artist Development

Education and mentorship opportunities for artists who want to engage with heritage and new technologies. Funded time to work on new ideas and projects, encouraging collaboration and skill-sharing internationally.

Equipment and Space

A free library of equipment needed for working in XR and immersive technologies. Free studio space for emerging artists and creatives in Portsmouth.

Authentic Experiences

Curating and producing artworks and experiences that foster a sense of empathy and community, leading to greater cohesion and social wellbeing.

Artist Development

Resonate

Play Office hosts Resonate — an artist development programme supporting emerging artists to engage with technology in their practice and gain creative and professional support in developing their careers in the arts.

Artists supported include Harry Payne, Hannah Mae Buckingham, James Wylie, Lara Kobeissi, and more.
