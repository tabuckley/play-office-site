Equipment library 2025

Borrow creative technology for free.

Play Office’s equipment library helps artists, students and community groups test ideas without buying expensive kit. Through our partnership with Share Portsmouth and the Library of Things, people can access tools for VR, projection, sound, sensors, 3D capture and immersive media.

VR Meta Quest headsets for immersive experiences and 3D spatial design.
Projection Portable projectors for mapping, installation and live performance.
Sound Field recorders, microphones, mixers and speakers for audio work.
Sensors Kinect, motion capture and environmental sensing tools.
3D Capture Photogrammetry rigs and LiDAR scanning for 3D digitisation.
Lighting LED panels, gels and rigging for photography and installation.
Creative Coding Arduinos, Raspberry Pis and electronics for interactive work.
Installation Cables, stands, mounts and technical kit for gallery-ready work.

Artist development 2023–2025
“Resonate has given me the opportunity to elevate my practice beyond one medium — essentially giving me the key to a door I didn’t know could open.”

Resonate is Play Office’s artist development programme for artists experimenting with immersive media, XR, digital storytelling and new forms of public engagement. It provides paid development time, technical support, mentorship and international exhibition opportunities.
