Tech. Library — Borrow equipment for free 2025

Borrow creative technology for free

Play Office's equipment library helps artists, students and community groups test ideas without needing to buy expensive kit. Through our partnership with Share Portsmouth and the Library of Things, people can access tools for VR, projection, sound, sensors, 3D capture and immersive media.

Artist Development — Resonate 2025

Supporting artists to make new work with technology

Resonate is Play Office's artist development programme for artists experimenting with immersive media, XR, digital storytelling and new forms of public engagement. The programme combines mentoring, peer support, practical access to equipment and space, commissioning, public outcomes and international connection.

12 Artist alumni
