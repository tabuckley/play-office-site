About Play Office 2025

Play Office aims to explore local heritage as inspiration for placemaking and artistic works. Play Office is an incubation space for creatives to develop dynamic, future-focused, responsive practices.

Artist Development

Education and mentorship opportunities to artists who want to engage with Heritage and New Technologies. Funded time to work on new ideas and projects. Encouraging collaboration and skill-sharing internationally.

Equipment and Space

A free library of equipment needed for working in XR and immersive technologies. Free studio space for emerging artists and creatives in Portsmouth.

Authentic Experiences

Curate and produce artworks and experiences that foster a sense of empathy and community leading to greater cohesion and social wellbeing.

Artist Development — Resonate (ii) Artist Cohort 2025

Play Office hosts Resonate — an artist development programme supporting emerging artists to engage with technology in their practice and gain both creative and professional support in developing their careers in the arts.
