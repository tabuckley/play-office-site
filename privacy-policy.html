Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy for Play Office, operated by Thomas Buckley (trading as Play Office). We take the privacy of everyone who contacts, visits or participates in Play Office seriously.

This policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

Who we are

Play Office is a free creative technology lab, equipment library and artist studio based in Portsmouth, UK. It is directed by Thomas Buckley and supported by Sustainable Conservation Trust.

Contact: info@thomas-buckley.com

What information we may collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us, sign up for a workshop or take part in a programme:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your organisation or affiliation, if relevant
  • Information you provide when applying for or participating in programmes like Resonate
  • Photographs or documentation from events and workshops, where you have consented
  • Monitoring and evaluation data for funded programmes (collected with your consent and in line with funder requirements)

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or analytics tools. If this changes, we will update this policy.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and communicate with you about Play Office activities
  • Organise and deliver workshops, programmes and events you have signed up for
  • Meet reporting requirements for funded programmes (for example, reporting to Arts Council England)
  • Understand who is using Play Office and how to improve our offer

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except where required to fulfil a service you have requested or where required by law.

How long we keep your data

We keep your data only for as long as is necessary for the purpose it was collected. For funded programme reporting, data may be retained for the period required by funders. Contact data for general enquiries is retained for no longer than 2 years unless you have an ongoing relationship with Play Office.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal and funder requirements)
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@thomas-buckley.com.

Images and documentation

Play Office may take photographs or video at workshops and events for documentation and promotional purposes. We will always ask for your consent before using images in which you are identifiable. You may withdraw consent at any time.

Links to other websites

This website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

This policy was last updated June 2025.
